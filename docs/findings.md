Findings and Observations

Candidate Quality

The dataset contained a large variety of profiles across multiple domains.

Examples:

* AI Engineers
* ML Engineers
* NLP Engineers
* Search Engineers
* Recommendation Systems Engineers
* Data Scientists

⸻

Importance of Recruiter Signals

Behavioral signals proved highly useful.

Most useful signals:

* GitHub Activity Score
* Recruiter Response Rate
* Notice Period
* Open To Work Flag

Candidates with strong behavioral signals were generally more attractive hiring prospects.

⸻

Retrieval Skills Were Highly Predictive

The strongest candidates frequently possessed:

* Embeddings
* Information Retrieval
* Recommendation Systems
* Semantic Search
* BM25
* Pinecone
* Qdrant
* Weaviate
* OpenSearch
* FAISS

These skills aligned closely with the target role.

⸻

Candidate Verification

Manual inspection was performed on top-ranked candidates.

Observations:

* Experience levels were realistic
* Skills matched experience
* Strong recruiter engagement metrics
* High GitHub activity

No obvious honeypot candidates were identified among the final shortlisted profiles.

⸻

Notice Period Impact

Notice period proved important.

Candidates with:

* 15 days notice
* 30 days notice

were generally more attractive than candidates requiring 90–120 days.

⸻

GitHub Activity Impact

GitHub Activity Score helped identify technically active candidates.

High scoring candidates often demonstrated:

* Modern AI tooling
* Retrieval technologies
* Open-source engagement

⸻

Final Observation

The best results were achieved by combining:

* Job title relevance
* Technical skill matching
* Recruiter behavioral signals
* Experience requirements

rather than relying on a single factor.
